A Slice of Togetherness

Posted by Tanbin Rownak Jahan on November 28, 2025

Each cake is a flower that grows at SugarBloom Bakery. Our edible art is a gain-on-demand, homemade, and made-with-love item to make your experience something you will never forget.

I have always liked cakes. They are tasty and all of them bring some pleasure. I like the fact that a baked good can make even an ordinary event something recognizable.

I would like to visit different cakes such as plain sponge cakes as well as the layered birthday cakes with cream and fresh fruits. I also prefer learning about new designs and techniques.

I do not just consider cakes as desserts. They entail partying, imagination, generosity and hospitality. They bring people together.
